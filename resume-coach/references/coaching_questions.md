# Coaching Questions Bank

Organized questions to help users discover and articulate resume content.

## By Achievement Type

### Cost Savings / Efficiency

```
- "Did you reduce costs anywhere? By how much?"
- "Did you eliminate any manual processes?"
- "Did you reduce the time something took? From how long to how long?"
- "Did you find and fix any wasteful spending?"
- "Were you able to do more with less? Fewer people, smaller budget?"
- "Did you renegotiate any contracts or vendor relationships?"
```

### Revenue / Growth

```
- "Did anything you did lead to more sales or revenue?"
- "Did you help acquire new customers? How many?"
- "Did you increase retention or reduce churn?"
- "Did you expand into new markets or segments?"
- "Did you launch new products or features that generated revenue?"
- "Did you influence pricing or packaging decisions?"
```

### Quality / Reliability

```
- "Did you reduce errors or defects? By what percentage?"
- "Did you improve uptime or reliability? From X% to Y%?"
- "Did you implement testing or quality processes?"
- "Did you reduce customer complaints or support tickets?"
- "Did you improve accuracy of something? Reports, forecasts, data?"
- "Did you pass any audits or achieve certifications?"
```

### Speed / Performance

```
- "Did you make something faster? How much faster?"
- "Did you reduce latency, load times, or response times?"
- "Did you speed up a process? Hiring, deployments, approvals?"
- "Did you reduce cycle time or time-to-market?"
- "Did you implement automation that saved time?"
```

### Scale / Volume

```
- "How many users/customers did your work affect?"
- "What was the data volume you worked with?"
- "How many transactions, requests, or events per day/month?"
- "How large was the codebase, system, or infrastructure?"
- "How many integrations, APIs, or services did you work with?"
```

### Leadership / People

```
- "How many people did you manage, lead, or mentor?"
- "Did you hire anyone? How many?"
- "Did you improve team performance or morale?"
- "Did you reduce turnover or improve retention?"
- "Did you develop anyone who got promoted?"
- "Did you lead any cross-functional initiatives?"
```

## By Role Level

```

- "What technologies did you learn or master in this role?"
- "Did you architect or design any systems?"
- "Did you optimize performance of anything?"

COLLABORATION:
- "Who did you work with outside your immediate team?"
- "Did you help onboard new team members?"
- "Did you present your work to stakeholders?"
- "Did you influence technical decisions?"
```

### Manager / Team Lead

```
PEOPLE:
- "How large was your team? Did it grow under you?"
- "How did you develop your team members?"
- "How did you handle underperformers?"

- "How did you handle competing stakeholder demands?"

STRATEGY:
```

### Executive / Director

## Probing for Missing Metrics

When users say "I don't have metrics":

```
ESTIMATE PROMPTS:
- "What was the rough order of magnitude? Thousands? Millions?"
- "What percentage improvement would you estimate?"
- "How much time did this save per week? Per person?"
- "If you had to guess, what's a conservative estimate?"
- "What would the business impact be if this failed?"

ALTERNATIVE METRICS:
- "If not dollars, what about users or customers affected?"
- "If not percentage, can we say 'X times faster'?"
- "Can we describe scope? 'Across 15 microservices' or 'serving 50K DAU'?"
- "What about before/after comparisons without exact numbers?"

VALIDATION:
- "Is this something you could verify if you needed to?"
- "Would your manager agree with this estimate?"
- "Are there any internal reports or dashboards that had this data?"
```

## Discovering Hidden Experience

```
SIDE PROJECTS:
- "Any personal projects that used similar skills?"
- "Open source contributions? Even small ones?"
- "Any technical blogs, talks, or tutorials?"

VOLUNTEER/PRO BONO:
- "Any volunteer work that used professional skills?"
- "Helped any nonprofits or community organizations?"
- "Mentoring or teaching outside of work?"

PREVIOUS CAREERS:
- "Skills from previous roles that transfer?"
- "Industries you worked in before tech?"
- "Customer-facing experience that's now valuable?"

EDUCATION/TRAINING:
- "Any recent certifications or courses?"
- "Bootcamps, online programs, or self-study?"
- "Hackathons, competitions, or workshops?"
```
