# Industry-Specific Coaching Prompts

Tailored questions for different industries to help surface relevant achievements and skills.

## Technology / Software Engineering

### Technical Depth
- "What's the most complex system you've designed or significantly contributed to?"
- "Have you worked on any systems handling high traffic or large data volumes?"
- "What's your experience with production incidents? Any notable saves?"
- "Have you introduced any new technologies or practices to your team?"

### Scale & Impact
- "How many users/requests did your systems serve?"
- "What's the largest codebase you've worked in?"
- "Any experience with distributed systems, microservices, or data pipelines?"
- "Have you worked on systems with strict reliability requirements (SLAs)?"

### Collaboration
- "Have you mentored other engineers or led technical discussions?"
- "Experience working with product managers to define requirements?"
- "Any cross-team initiatives you drove or participated in?"

### Keywords to Surface
`distributed systems, microservices, APIs, cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure), CI/CD,
observability, Kubernetes, data pipelines, machine learning, performance optimization`

## Finance / Banking

### Risk & Compliance
- "Have you worked on any regulatory compliance initiatives (SOX, GDPR, Basel)?"
- "Experience with risk modeling or stress testing?"
- "Any audit preparation or remediation work?"

### Quantitative Impact
- "What was the AUM or transaction volume you worked with?"
- "Any experience optimizing trading systems or reducing latency?"
- "Have you built or improved any financial models?"

### Client & Business
- "Experience with client-facing reporting or dashboards?"
- "Any work on portfolio optimization or investment analysis?"
- "Cross-functional work with traders, analysts, or operations?"

### Keywords to Surface
`risk management, regulatory compliance, AUM, P&L, trading systems,
financial modeling, Bloomberg, FIX protocol, quantitative analysis`

---

## Healthcare / Life Sciences

### Patient Impact
- "How did your work affect patient outcomes or care quality?"
- "Any experience with clinical workflows or EHR systems?"
- "Work on patient safety or quality improvement initiatives?"

### Regulatory & Compliance
- "Experience with HIPAA, FDA, or other healthcare regulations?"

### Data & Research
- "Work with clinical data, real-world evidence, or outcomes research?"

- "Experience with interoperability standards (HL7, FHIR)?"

### Keywords to Surface
`HIPAA, EHR/EMR, clinical workflows, FDA, patient outcomes,
population health, HL7/FHIR, clinical trials, quality improvement`

## General Probing Questions (All Industries)

### Impact
- "What would have happened if you hadn't done this?"
- "Who else cared about this outcome and why?"
- "Was this recognized formally (awards, promotions, mentions)?"

### Scale
- "How many people, dollars, or systems were affected?"
- "Was this a one-time effort or ongoing?"
- "What was the timeline and any deadline pressure?"

### Difficulty
- "What made this hard? What obstacles did you overcome?"
- "Did you have to convince anyone or change minds?"
- "What did you have to learn to accomplish this?"
